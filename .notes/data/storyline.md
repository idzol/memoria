# Memoria: Eight-Chapter Story Outline

This outline details the journey of Fisher (Perseus) as he recovers his shattered identity and confronts the Titaness of Memory.

## Chapter 1: The Shallows of Seriphos

Narrative Arc: "Fisher" lives a simple life under the care of Dictys (char_007). While scavenging the Jagged Reefs (area_1_2), he finds the Fisherman's Hook (item_001). Touching the Ark of Danae (area_1_4) triggers a traumatic flashback of a golden storm and a mother's scream, forcing him to realize his life is a fabrication.

* Key Areas: area_1_1 (Fisherman's Cove), area_1_4 (The Shifting Tidepool).
* Key Characters: char_007 (Dictys), char_019 (Glaucus).
* Key Items: item_001 (Harpe), item_005 (Ark of Danae), item_010 (Anchor of Danae).

## Chapter 2: The Whispering Woods of Ethiops

Narrative Arc: Following a shimmering Echo (char_003) into the woods, Perseus discovers a forest of petrified souls. He faces the Cetus (char_016) at the Shore of Sacrifice (area_2_2). By saving the Echo, he remembers his name for the first time: Perseus. The Mirror-Polished Lid (item_002) is found, revealing the face of Athena (char_006) in its surface.

* Key Areas: area_2_1 (Shadow Grove), area_2_2 (Shore of Sacrifice).
* Key Characters: char_003 (Andromeda), char_016 (Cetus), char_017 (Cassiopeia).
* Key Items: item_002 (Aegis), item_017 (Chains of Sacrifice).

## Chapter 3: The Caverns of the Graeae

Narrative Arc: To find the location of the Gorgons, Perseus descends into the Hall of Whispers (area_3_1). He outwits The Hags (char_009) in total darkness. Stealing the Eye of the Graeae (item_006) grants him "True Sight," allowing him to see the silver threads of Mnemosyne (char_002) that have been tethering his mind.

* Key Areas: area_3_2 (Chamber of the Single Eye), area_3_3 (Styx Tributary).
* Key Characters: char_009 (The Graeae), char_002 (Mnemosyne).
* Key Items: item_006 (Eye of the Graeae), item_011 (Helm of Darkness).

## Chapter 4: The Gardens of the Hesperides

Narrative Arc: Perseus ascends to the floating Golden Orchard (area_4_1). He meets The Vagabond (char_004), who reveals himself as Hermes. After racing the North Wind (char_020) across the Zephyr Cliffs (area_4_2), Perseus earns the Talaria (item_003) and the Kibisis (item_004), completing his heroic arsenal.

* Key Areas: area_4_1 (Golden Orchard), area_4_2 (Zephyr Cliffs).
* Key Characters: char_004 (Hermes), char_013 (The Hesperides), char_020 (Boreas).
* Key Items: item_003 (Talaria), item_004 (Kibisis), item_013 (Caduceus Fragment).

Narrative Arc: On the obsidian slopes of the Mirror Lair (area_5_2), Perseus faces his most painful memory: the slaying of Medusa (char_008). He realizes she was a victim of the gods, and his "victory" was also a tragedy. By accepting this guilt, the Severed Head (item_012) is restored as a tool of justice, and Pegasus (char_014) is born from the memory-mist.

* Key Areas: area_5_2 (Mirror Lair), area_5_3 (Peak of Rebirth).
* Key Characters: char_008 (Medusa), char_014 (Pegasus).
* Key Items: item_012 (Head of Medusa), item_018 (Pegasus Quill).

## Chapter 6: The Hall of the Petrified King

Narrative Arc: Perseus returns to his home in Argos, but it is a silent, frozen tomb. In the Gallery of Statues (area_6_2), he confronts the memory of Polydectes (char_011) and frees his mother, Danae (char_010), from a golden prison. He rejects the throne of stone to pursue the Titaness, reclaiming his strategic brilliance through the Owl Mask (item_015).

* Key Areas: area_6_2 (Gallery of Statues), area_6_3 (Tower of Danae).
* Key Characters: char_010 (Danae), char_011 (Polydectes), char_012 (Acrisius).
* Key Items: item_015 (Visage of the Strategist), item_009 (Fragment of Atlas).

## Chapter 7: The Sky-Pillar Plateau

Narrative Arc: Climbing to the top of the world, Perseus speaks with the mountain-giant Atlas (char_005). He uses the Thunderbolt Shard (item_019) to clear the frozen paths of the Frost-Bite Ridge (area_7_8). He learns that Atlas helped Mnemosyne to punish Perseus for turning him to stone; Perseus offers a gesture of mercy, convincing Atlas to let him pass.

* Key Areas: area_7_2 (Shoulder of the Giant), area_7_10 (Gate of Tartarus).
* Key Characters: char_005 (Atlas), char_014 (Pegasus).
* Key Items: item_009 (Fragment of Atlas), item_019 (Thunderbolt Shard).

## Chapter 8: The Well of Mnemosyne

Narrative Arc: Perseus enters the Silver Conflux (area_8_1), the metaphysical center of all memory. The Weaver (char_002) attempts to overwrite his soul with a blank history. Perseus utilizes all 20 divine items, calling upon the spectral support of Glaucus (char_019) and Andromeda (char_003). He defeats the Titaness by choosing to remember both his glory and his pain, shattering the labyrinth.

* Key Areas: area_8_1 (Silver Conflux), area_8_3 (Heart of the Weaver).
* Key Characters: char_002 (Mnemosyne), char_001 (Perseus), char_015 (Zeus).
* Key Items: All Items (True States fully unlocked).
